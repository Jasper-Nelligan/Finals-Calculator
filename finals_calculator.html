Finals Calculator

This tool will calculate what you need on your final exam in order to pass the course. Simply input the passing grade for the course,
then each assignments grade and weight in percent, and finally the weight of the final exam. The calculator will then tell you what
percent you need to pass! If you haven't gotten marks backs for some assignments, that's okay too. Just input the assignments weight
but leave the grade field blank. The calculator will return the average you need on all remaining assignments in order to pass.
